IV. Also accepted rules:

  • Absolutely no discussion of cheating or exploiting the game. Bans for this will be handed out unless it's arguably a bug report, and will also be retroactive.

  • Adult content: #Bonerseer when requested, and when appropriate.

  • Note: Keep in mind that we may divulge into The Adult Topics (like taxes) occasionally. If at any time you feel uncomfortable, we encourage you to ask for the conversation to be moved to #Bonerseer. Do not feel bad for speaking up about your grievances. We will not attack you.

  • This memo is mostly for Overseer-related topics, so try not to get off-topic too badly. You can always take topics on other conversations to a separate memo or private discussion

  • Although we are not an RP memo. DO NOT RP.

  • Keeping the peace: No fighting. Pulling out imaginary guns in order to force someone else into submission is pointless on the internet. The people in the memo are far more productive when utilized as friends.

  • Spammers will die a quick and painful death.

  • Moderators: If you have a problem, talk to a mod. Have a pesterlog ready.

    • We aim for warning > kick(s) > ban (temporary). Contest ban with other moderators.

    • Permanent bans are rare, but will occur on exceptional or repeated offenses 

    • If you have something you feel you cannot say in the memo, feel free to talk to the moderators privately. Anything you say or do will be held confidential. We respect your privacy.

    • If it’s an on-going situation, give every detail of the situation. Twisting tales is not tolerated.

  • Quirks: Quirks are allowed, as is testing of quirks (unless general consensus of memo is "no").

  • Gradients are allowed. However, please respect that not everyone has Pesterchum, and therefore cannot read your gradients. Putting sentences in (( double parentheses )) or [[ in double brackets ]] removes the quirk/gradient for the chosen text.

    • Please keep raging about muted quirks to a minimum.

    • Note: If using a gradient and an IRC user asks you to disable, be curteous and do so. It is virtually incomprehensible on IRC. Also, if your quirk is unreadable, people might ask you to disable that too. If you argue, a moderator has final say. 

    • If you STILL don't disable your quirk after a moderator asks you to, quirks will just be turned off in the memo. 

  • Non-pesterchum users: One person is allowed to go without initials when using extraneous clients. First come, first serve. Second extraneous client user must use initials to distinguish. 

    • You can go the full route and enter PESTERCHUM:TIME>i (for current), PESTERCHUM:TIME>P##:## (past), PESTERCHUM:TIME>F##:## (future) and paste <c=#,#,#>II (example, <c=1,15,255>PP:) before everything you say, making it visible to chumdroid users, or

    • You can also go the easy route and type your initials before everything you type (example, PP:). This is not visible to chumdroid, and merely serves as a way to distinguish speakers.

  • Some people do not appreciate being impersonated. Refusing to respect that will result in a temporary ban.

  • Handles: No racist, sexist, or potentially triggering handles. This will be handled with a kick. Repeated offenses may be punished with a temporary ban.

    • Regardless of intent, most are not likely to find it as amusing. Please understand.

    • Multiple handles held by the same person at one time in the memo are allowed, within reason.

    • Bots are allowed, but restricted.

      • Responses cannot occur more often than 7:1 (ie: 7 messages from others to every 1 message from the bot)

    •  If you're entering under a new handle, we'd really appreciate it if you identified yourself with us.;; You don't have to, everyone has their own reasons.

  • No item spamming. Do not link your submissions simply to show off. Links are allowed if:

    • You wish to get assistance with balancing or otherwise

    • You want to bring it to the attention of the dev team as something that is problematic.

What we can disclose about the project:

  • Code and recipe hints

  • What the best items are

  • How much grist something is

  • Bugs

  • Where Overseer lives

  • What Blah looks like

CODE SHARING AND RECIPE SHARING IS NOT ALLOWED. Code sharing is forbidden by requests of the devs, as is giving straight up recipes. You can give hints only. 

Grist giving, while frowned upon by the devs, is not forbidden, within reason - only sensible amounts are allowed to be given. No endgame amounts. A general rule is if you're asking for more than 3 million total, you shouldn't be. Play the damn game instead. You're also limited to three grist requests per week. Additionally, we ask that you've been a regular in the memo for at least a week before asking, since too many people show up, ask for grist, then disappear until they next need some.
