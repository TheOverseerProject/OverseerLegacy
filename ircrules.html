GENERAL IRC RULES

- First and foremost, take responsibility for your actions!

- No discussion of illegal content is allowed on the network, for obvious reasons.


OVERSEER SPECIFIC RULES


- Please don't discuss ways to cheat in the game. Bans for this will be handed out by whichever admin sees it.

- Adult content – Not everyone is over 18, so in keeping with the traditions of the Pesterchum memo, move any discussion of that to the channel #Bonerseer

- Keep topics associated with SJWs out of the chats please. While we know the Homestuck fandom is full of people who are active about it, not everyone wants to hear about those topics.

- These are not RP rooms, though you're free to make other rooms on the server to do this!

- Don't spam

- If you have a problem, keep a log handy – moderators will help you sort it out!

- People don't appreciate being impersonated. Don't steal handles - we'd prefer you stuck to the one you use in game. Feel free to use NickServ to register your name!

- No item spamming – don't link your submissions just to show off!


- CODE SHARING AND RECIPE SHARING IS NOT ALLOWED. Code sharing is forbidden by request of the dev team, as is giving recipes. Please give hints only.

We're not particularly fond of grist giving either but we can't exactly stop it. However, sensible amounts only – no asking for quantities in the millions.
